Translating MAHALO License

Sun 12 June 2011
By Yusaku ONO

In content.

MAHALO License is a software license defined by @gongoZ.

@gongoZ is a developer of the Tython programming language. He distributes Tython with the MAHALO License.

MAHALO License is based on MIT License and is added the below sentence.

ソフトウェアを使用する際、(ソフトウェアに限らず)何かに対して「感謝」をすることとします。

I'm trying to translate this sentence into English. It's a draft version.

You shall thank to anyone (not only to the software) when you use the software.
